Mina System Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-04

Mina System is a materials inventory and custody management system for companies, warehouses, workshops, maintenance teams, and industrial operations.

Contact

For privacy, support, or account requests, contact adlymina99@gmail.com.

Data handled by Mina System

Mina System may handle account details, company profile data, company membership and role data, worker records, tool records, custody transactions, signatures, uploaded images or documents, generated reports, and audit or security records.

Purpose of use

Data is used to provide authentication, company workspaces, role-based access, inventory custody workflows, reporting, accountability, support, security, and troubleshooting.

Service providers

Mina System may use Supabase for authentication, database, storage, and backend services. Brevo or another configured email provider may be used for authentication and support email delivery.

Sharing

Mina System does not sell user data. Data may be processed by service providers only as needed to operate the app and provide support.

Retention and account requests

Operational records may be retained while a company account is active. Verified account requests may result in deletion or anonymization of account profile data, unless limited retention is required for operational, security, audit, contractual, or legal reasons.

Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Mina System changes. Material updates should be reflected on this page.
